Cheetahas!

Appearance:Cheetahs are golden with black spots.They have black lines across their face that block the sunlight.Cheetahs have a flexible backbone and long legs so they are super fast

Predators: Lions hunt cheetahs. They also steal cheetahs prey

Prey: Cheetahs favorite prey is gazelle.Since gazelle run fast cheetahs evolved to be super fast

Habitat: Cheetahs live in the savanna which is a perfect place for sprinting.It has miles of grass with very few trees

Camoflauge: Cheetah cubs have backs that look like badgurs fur because lions are scared of badgurs so lions will leave them alone when there napping.

Sound:Cheetahs make a bird like chirp to communicate to their young
